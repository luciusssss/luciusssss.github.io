  Research Interests

Currently, my research interests include

  • NLP for Low-Resource Languages: Enhancing the transparency, inclusivity, and efficiency in human language technology for underrepresented languages.
  • LLMs for Language Science: Leveraging LLMs to model linguistic phenomena, such as second-language acquisition and language contact, for deeper insights into how human languages function and evolve.
  • Complex Reasoning with LLMs: Harnessing the potential of LLMs to tackle complex problems and building trustworthy systems for specialized domains such as law and linguistics.
